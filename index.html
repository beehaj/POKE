WHO ARE WE

At Poke, we are a storytelling consultancy, born out of the belief that great brands emerge when products are immersed in great stories. We believe this is the difference between selling a bottle of carbonated sugar water… and a bottle of happiness. So, our job is to help brands transform their business goals into well-told stories, across multiple platforms. Taking the same traditional story arcs that have existed for centuries and turning it into a new creative science. We believe in Heroes, Quests, Enemies, Allies, and Goals; a formula that has proved highly successful for our clients. This is why we believe we are what happens when storytelling and science meet.
POKE; the storytelling consultancy.

BUSINESS GOAL When young people enjoy their Indomie recipes they eat more, so how do you get them interested in Indomie recipes during the African Nations Cup season?

IDEA The African Plate of Nations, every participating African nation in the nation’s cup represented as their national dishes, reimagined as Indomie recipes. So when teams go head-to-head on the pitch, their dishes go head-to-head on our page. Showing the fun dexterity of Indomie as a product that can be consumed in so many exciting ways during the competition.
